Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

To create an account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
  3. Choose your account type (Individual, Business, or Enterprise)
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. Check your email for verification (if required)

Once registered, you can access your dashboard and manage your IoT devices.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Sign In page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Setting up your Tecosoft IoT gateway:

  1. Unbox and Connect: Connect power and ethernet cables
  2. Initial Setup: Access the web interface at the device IP
  3. Network Configuration: Configure WiFi or ethernet settings
  4. Device Registration: Register with your Tecosoft account
  5. Sensor Connection: Connect your industrial sensors
  6. Data Configuration: Set up data collection parameters

Detailed setup guides are available in our documentation section.

Troubleshooting connection issues:

  1. Check Power: Ensure device is properly powered
  2. Network Status: Verify network connectivity
  3. Firewall Settings: Check if ports are blocked
  4. Device Status: Look for LED indicators
  5. Restart Device: Power cycle the gateway
  6. Contact Support: If issues persist, contact our technical team

For immediate assistance, use our live chat or call our support hotline.

Accessing your industrial data analytics:

  1. Log into your Tecosoft dashboard
  2. Navigate to "Analytics" section
  3. Select your device or facility
  4. Choose time range and metrics
  5. View real-time and historical data
  6. Export reports as needed

Our analytics platform provides insights into efficiency, predictive maintenance, and operational optimization.
